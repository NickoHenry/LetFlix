History

Our journey started from a DVD Rental Service based at Alam Sutera, Jakarta. Throughout the time, we wanted to expand our business with creating web – based rental and sales site where our customer can rent any DVD available through mail. With the technology keep on developing, we successfully to created a movie recommendation system for our dear customer on our website. Therefore, the idea of LeTFliX was born until now become the largest streaming platform in the world with thousands of dramas that user can watch unlimitedly
